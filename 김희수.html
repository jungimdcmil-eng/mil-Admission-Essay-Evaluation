Part C: Writing Corrections

Sentence: "In introduction, I like country named is Japan."

Correction:

In the introduction, I will talk about a country named Japan, which I like.

'I like'와 'is' 동사가 두 번 쓰여서 어색해요. 'named Japan'은 'country'를 꾸며주는 말이므로, 문장 구조를 바로잡았어요.

Suggested Improvement (G3):

For my introduction, I want to talk about Japan, which is my favorite country to visit.

'내가 가장 좋아하는 나라'라고 표현하면 글의 주제를 더 명확하게 보여줄 수 있어요.

Sentence: "First, I like Japan because they has the curtral there are going to beautiful temples, going to themepark or waterpark, and seeing a swim."

Correction:

First, I like Japan because it has many cultural activities, like going to beautiful temples, visiting theme parks or waterparks, and watching sumo wrestling.

'Japan'은 단수이므로 'they has'가 아니라 'it has'를 써야 해요. 'curtral'의 철자를 'cultural'로 고치고, 활동들을 나열하는 방식으로 문장을 정리했어요.

Suggested Improvement (G3):

The first reason I like Japan is for its rich culture. There are many activities to enjoy, such as visiting beautiful temples, having fun at theme parks, and watching exciting sumo wrestling matches.

문단의 중심 생각을 'rich culture(풍부한 문화)'라고 명확히 밝히고, 활동들을 예시로 설명하면 글이 더 체계적으로 보여요.

Sentence: "second, I like Japan because of it have a beautiful jews."

Correction:

Second, I like Japan because it has beautiful views.

'Japan'은 단수이므로 'it has'를 써야 해요. 'jews(유대인)'는 문맥상 'views(경치)'를 잘못 쓴 것으로 보여 수정했어요.

Suggested Improvement (G3):

Another reason I like Japan is because of its beautiful scenery.

'경치'를 의미하는 'scenery'라는 단어를 사용하면 더 정확하게 표현할 수 있어요.

Part D: Final Revised Essay

Why I Love to Visit Japan

(Introduction) There are many countries to visit in the world, but my favorite is Japan. It is a place filled with amazing sights and fun things to do. I love Japan because it has a rich culture with many exciting activities and it is full of beautiful scenery.

(Body Paragraph 1) First, I enjoy Japan because there are many wonderful cultural activities. My family and I can visit beautiful and peaceful temples that have flowers all around them. For more excitement, we can go to a theme park or a waterpark to ride fun rides. I also find it fascinating to watch sumo wrestling, which is a traditional Japanese sport. Plus, the cultural food, like delicious sushi that my mom and I ate, is a special treat. These are all my favorite things to do.

(Body Paragraph 2) Second, Japan is a great place because of its beautiful views and nature. I really like camping because I love being outdoors. When we go camping, we can see many colorful flowers and enjoy the fresh air. Another amazing sight is the sea view. When you look out at the ocean, you can see the deep blue sea stretching far away. Even the temples have great views and are surrounded by lovely nature.

(Conclusion) In conclusion, Japan is a fantastic place for a trip. I like it so much because of its interesting culture and beautiful views. From the temples to the sea, there is always something amazing to see and do. I think if you visit Japan, you will be very excited and agree that it is a beautiful country.

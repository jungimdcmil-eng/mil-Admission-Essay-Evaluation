Sentence Structure

Beginner (G4)

Essay Structure

Intermediate (G4-G5)

제안: 'P.E.E.L' (Point, Evidence, Explanation, Link) 구조를 사용하여 본론 문단을 작성하는 연습을 해야 합니다. 먼저 주제 문장(Point)을 쓰고, 그것을 뒷받침하는 구체적인 예시(Evidence)를 든 다음, 그 예시가 왜 중요한지 설명(Explanation)하는 훈련이 필요합니다.

Step 2: 어휘 선택 및 문장 흐름 교정

아이디어 구체화 부족 (Lack of Elaboration): 에세이의 가장 큰 약점은 각 본론 문단에 구체적인 예시나 설명이 없다는 것입니다. 주장은 있지만, 왜 그런지, 어떻게 그런지에 대한 설명이 부족하여 설득력이 떨어집니다.

제안 (Suggestion): 각 본론 문단마다 최소 한두 문장의 예시를 추가하는 연습이 필요합니다. 예를 들어, "영어를 사용해서 다른 나라 사람들과 대화할 수 있다"는 주장 뒤에, "For example, when I travel to America, I can use English to order food at a restaurant or ask for directions on the street." 와 같은 구체적인 상황을 덧붙여주면 글이 훨씬 풍부해집니다.

The Benefits of Learning English

There are three main benefits of learning English: it allows us to communicate globally, travel more easily, and access more information.

First, we can communicate with people from other countries. English is a widespread language, so many people around the world can speak it. For example, if I meet a new friend from the Philippines online, I can use English to share my hobbies and learn about their culture. This helps us to understand each other and build friendships across borders.

Second, learning English makes it easier to travel to other countries. When we visit a place like the United States or the United Kingdom, most signs, menus, and announcements are in English. For instance, if I get lost in a big city, I can use English to ask for directions. This makes traveling safer and more enjoyable because I can explore new places with confidence.

Third, knowing English helps us learn new things. A lot of interesting books, movies, and websites are in English. For example, I can read popular American comic books or watch famous Hollywood movies without subtitles. This opens up a whole new world of entertainment and knowledge that I couldn't access with only Korean.

In conclusion, learning English is very useful. It helps us make friends with people from different backgrounds, travel the world without fear, and enjoy more books and movies. These are the three great benefits of learning English.
